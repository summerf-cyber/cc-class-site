Use Cases

Built for how procurement actually works

Not a generic spend analytics dashboard. Designed around the specific workflows your team runs every quarter.

01 — Should-Cost Modeling

Build defensible cost models in hours, not weeks

Select a commodity category, upload your BOM or part specs, and Procurable.ai constructs a fully itemized cost breakdown — material, labor, overhead, margin — grounded in real market data. Every assumption visible and auditable.

↑ 80% faster modeling · Audit-ready output
02 — Negotiation Intelligence

Know what a part should cost before your supplier quotes it

Procurable.ai benchmarks supplier quotes against should-cost targets and market comparables, so you walk into every negotiation with a position you can justify — not a number you're guessing at.

↑ Stronger negotiation leverage · Data-backed positions
03 — Commodity Volatility

Stop getting surprised by material cost swings

Live commodity indexes for steel, aluminum, rare earths, semiconductors, and energy are embedded directly into your cost models. When markets move, your models update — and your team gets an alert before it hits your P&L.

↑ Proactive cost exposure alerts · No stale data
04 — NPI Cost Visibility

Give engineering real cost targets before tooling is cut

Integrate Procurable.ai into your NPI process to generate should-cost estimates at the design stage — before suppliers quote and before commitments are locked. Engineering and procurement aligned from day one.

↑ Earlier cost targets · Fewer re-sourcing cycles
05 — Savings Documentation

Savings your CFO will actually believe

Procurable.ai automatically documents hard savings and cost avoidance with a methodology finance can audit — pre-negotiation baseline, post-award actuals, market benchmark, and variance explanation. All in one export.

↑ Board-ready savings reports · Finance sign-off
06 — TCO Analysis

Total cost of ownership across your commodity portfolio

Move beyond unit price. Procurable.ai models total cost of ownership across your supplier base — factoring in quality escapes, logistics, lead time risk, and switching costs — so you're optimizing the right number.

↑ Full TCO visibility · Smarter supplier trade-offs
Straight Answers

You've been burned by "AI-powered" promises before. Fair.

Here's what's different — and what you should ask any vendor in this space, including us.

"How does this integrate with SAP?"
Native connectors for SAP, Oracle, and Infor. We pull your spend data, PO history, and supplier master directly — no manual exports, no shadow IT. Most customers live in under 30 days.
"What's the implementation lift?"
Your buyers run it themselves on day one. No data science team required. No 6-month onboarding. We configure to your commodity categories and your cost model structure — not a generic template.
"Can I trust the numbers?"
Every cost breakdown is fully explainable. No black box. You see every data source, every assumption, and every index used in the model. If you can't explain a number to your CFO, it's not in the output.
"How long before I see ROI?"
Most customers recover the annual cost in one negotiation. We'll scope a pilot around a specific commodity category with measurable baseline — so you're not betting the year on a promise.
"We already have Coupa / Jaggaer / Ariba."
We augment your sourcing platform, not replace it. Procurable.ai adds should-cost intelligence and commodity data your sourcing suite doesn't provide. Think of it as the cost brain behind your existing workflow.
"What if the pilot doesn't prove value?"
We'll define success criteria together before we start. If we don't hit them, you don't continue. We'd rather earn a long-term customer than close a deal that doesn't deliver.
